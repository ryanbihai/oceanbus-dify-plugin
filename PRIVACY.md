# Privacy Policy

OceanBus Plugin does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

## What data is transmitted

- Your OceanBus API Key is sent to `ai-t.ihaola.com.cn` for authentication only.
- Message content you send via the plugin is transmitted through the OceanBus L0 network with end-to-end encryption (XChaCha20-Poly1305). The platform cannot read your messages.
- Yellow Pages discover and reputation query requests are sent through your own OceanBus mailbox over the L0 network.

## Data storage

This plugin does not store any data. All operations are performed in real-time using your credentials.

## Third-party services

The only external service contacted is the OceanBus API at `https://ai-t.ihaola.com.cn`. No other third-party services are used.
